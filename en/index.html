About

About the Event

A tennis social for Neuro2026 attendees. Researchers and students active in neuroscience are invited to connect across labs and fields through sport.

We plan to play on omnibus courts, so please bring omnibus-compatible tennis shoes if possible. We will provide balls; please bring your own racket if you can. You may be able to borrow one from participants who bring extras.

From competitive players to beginners and those returning after a break—everyone is welcome in a recreational format.
